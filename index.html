The Legend

From the Himalayas to Ironman Glory

Growing up in the shadows of the world's highest peaks, I learned that true strength comes from within. Every mountain trail, every challenging climb prepared me for the ultimate test of endurance.

Becoming Nepal's first Ironman wasn't just a personal achievement—it was a statement that champions can emerge from anywhere. Now I'm dedicated to sharing what I've learned and building a community of athletes who believe that limits are meant to be broken.
