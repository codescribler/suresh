The Legend

From the Himalayas to Ironman Glory

Growing up in the shadows of the world's highest peaks, I learned that true strength comes from within. Every mountain trail, every challenging climb prepared me for the ultimate test of endurance.

Becoming Nepal's first Ironman wasn't just a personal achievement—it was a statement that champions can emerge from anywhere. Now, I pour that same determination into every bike that bears my name.
